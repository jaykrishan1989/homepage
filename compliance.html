Operations Framework

Dronemark operates under the strict mandate of the Canadian Aviation Regulations (CARs) Part IX, ensuring institutional-grade safety and legal certainty in every mission.

Operational Category Advanced Operations Complex (SFOC) Operations
Airspace Access Controlled airspace (Class C, D, E) with NAV CANADA coordination. Restricted airspace or specialized zones requiring specific permits.
Proximity to People Within 30m horizontally of bystanders (down to 5m with safety tech). Directly over assembly of people or within restricted safety buffers.
Mission Scope Standard infrastructure inspection, urban surveying, and SAR. BVLOS (Beyond Visual Line of Sight) or high-altitude specialized missions.
Regulatory Trigger CAR 901.62 Compliance. Special Flight Operations Certificate (SFOC) required per mission.

Certified RPAS Professionals

Being "Certified RPAS" (Remotely Piloted Aircraft System) means our pilots have passed Transport Canada's advanced examinations and flight reviews. Unlike hobbyists, we are legally authorized to fly in busy city centers, near airports, and close to people.
